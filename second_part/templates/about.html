About Us

Understand Your Training With Data

Running Companion helps you analyze your training patterns and get insights from your data

Understanding the Analysis

This tool analyzes your Strava data to help understand your training patterns and effectiveness. It collects data about:

  • Running activities and patterns
  • Training load and intensity
  • Performance metrics and trends
  • Heart rate zones and distribution
  • Elevation profiles and performance factors

Data Privacy

Your data is handled securely and privately. Only your activity data is stored, with no personal identifying information except your Strava athlete ID.

We use industry-standard security practices to ensure your data remains protected and private.

Your data is never shared with third parties

How It Works

1. Connect

Connect your Strava account to give us access to your activities data.

2. Analyze

We process your running data to extract meaningful insights.

3. Visualize

View your data through clean, intuitive dashboards and charts.
